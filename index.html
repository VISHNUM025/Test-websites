Our Story

Founded in 2015, Demo Coffee & More has been serving the community with passion and dedication. We source our beans directly from farmers, ensuring fair trade and exceptional quality in every cup.

Our expert baristas craft each drink with precision, creating not just coffee, but experiences that bring people together. From our signature blends to artisanal pastries, everything is made with love.
